# Compliance review

Use this skill when work touches regulated data, audit trails, privacy, retention,
licensing, or organization policy.

## Workflow

1. Identify the applicable compliance concern.
2. Trace what data is collected, stored, displayed, shared, or deleted.
3. Check consent, retention, auditability, and access control assumptions.
4. Distinguish legal advice from engineering controls.
5. Recommend concrete engineering safeguards.
6. List unresolved policy questions.

## Checks

- Personal data handling is explicit.
- Retention and deletion behavior are understood.
- Access is least-privilege.
- Logs avoid sensitive data.
- Third-party dependency or license concerns are flagged.
- Audit trail requirements are considered.

## Output

Return:

- Compliance area
- Findings
- Engineering recommendations
- Open policy/legal questions
- Residual risk
