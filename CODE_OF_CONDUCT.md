# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep discussion technical, respectful, and useful.

Expected behavior:

- be respectful in disagreement
- assume good intent first
- focus on code, behavior, and evidence
- give actionable feedback
- avoid harassment, insults, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- personal attacks
- harassment
- discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull
requests, or contributions that do not meet this standard.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct issues privately to the project maintainer.
